Info

StarPractice helps students, educators and parents by providing easy ways of generating new practice questions and tracking completed work.

You can add/remove activities in the "Manage Activities" section. Afer you add a new activity, you can start practicing it immediately. When you no longer need that activity you can remove it from your application.

When you complete an activity by pressing "Finish Activity", history of this activity is automatically saved and can be reviewed later by going to "History".

In customiz versions of this service, educators are provided with additional functionalities:

  • create own tests
  • choose from provided question templates
  • tailor practice sets for individual students or groups
  • receive student responses

If you want to get these additional features, please contact info@sparkydots.com for more information.
