Info

EarnIt helps parents give their children math assignments, by allowing them to set up workable contracts with their children.

Whenever you work on an activity, you are earning credits - which are translated into dollars at the rate set by you parent.

When you complete an activity, history of this activity is automatically saved and can be reviewed later by going to "History".

If you want to get these additional features, please contact info@sparkydots.com for more information.
